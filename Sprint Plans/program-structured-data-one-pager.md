# Executive Summary

Over the past year, Seattle University’s web platform has undergone a major technical search optimization initiative focused on improving how our academic programs are understood by modern search engines and AI-powered discovery systems.

This work has significantly strengthened the university’s visibility in Google Search and positioned Seattle University as a leader in higher education for **AI-ready web infrastructure and structured data transparency.**

As a result of these improvements, Seattle University’s average Google search position improved dramatically—from rankings in the **50s in 2020** to **page-one visibility in the teens today.**

This work ensures that prospective students, families, and partners can more easily discover Seattle University programs through Google, AI search systems, and emerging knowledge-graph-based discovery tools.

---

# Strategic Impact

The initiative focuses on making Seattle University’s academic programs **machine-readable and semantically structured**, allowing search engines to understand our programs as authoritative educational entities rather than simple webpages.

Key strategic outcomes include:

- Improved Google search rankings and visibility
- Increased program discoverability for prospective students
- Enhanced compatibility with Google’s **AI Overviews and Knowledge Graph**
- Stronger representation of Seattle University in search results
- Long-term resilience as search technology evolves

This work positions Seattle University ahead of many peer institutions in **technical SEO maturity and structured data adoption.**

---

# Key Technical Achievements

### Structured Data Architecture
Implemented advanced structured data for all academic program pages using the Schema.org **EducationalOccupationalProgram** standard.

This allows search engines to understand:

- Program names and credentials
- Degree outcomes and career pathways
- Time to completion and credits
- Program format (online, in-person, hybrid)
- Associated occupations and industries

---

### Institutional Authority Signals

Added structured **provider information** identifying Seattle University as the authoritative institution behind each program.

This strengthens Google’s understanding of Seattle University as a trusted academic entity within its Knowledge Graph.

---

### Program FAQ Framework

Developed a scalable **Program FAQ system** within the university CMS.

Benefits include:

- Improved search results visibility
- Better support for AI-generated answers
- Clearer information for prospective students

---

### Metadata & Search Preview Enhancements

Expanded Open Graph and metadata coverage across program pages to improve:

- Google search snippets
- Social media previews
- AI indexing signals
